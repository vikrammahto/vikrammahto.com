Hey there! 👋 I'm **Vikram**, a self-taught web developer from a small town in India. I currently work full-time as a **Frontend Engineer** - and moonlight as a dreamer, builder, and freelancer-in-progress.

My superpower? I **craft clean, accessible, and lightning-fast UIs** that feel as good as they look.

### 🚀 What I Do

- Develop pixel-perfect, performant UIs using **React**, **Next.js**, and **Tailwind CSS**
- Prioritize **accessibility** and **web standards** - semantic HTML, ARIA
- Build blazing-fast, SEO-optimized, mobile-first sites with a focus on **Core Web Vitals**
- Ship side projects like [PaisaSync – a personal finance tracker](https://github.com/vikrammahto/paisasync)
- Design with empathy - always thinking about the **end user experience**

### 🛤️ My Journey So Far

No fancy bootcamp. Just a laptop, endless curiosity, and lots of late nights breaking stuff.

From writing spaghetti JS to architecting full-stack SaaS apps - it’s been a grind. Along the way, I faced:

- Burnout, rejections, and imposter syndrome
- Money mistakes and debt pressure
- Self-doubt and overthinking

But I kept showing up. Learning, failing, improving - brick by brick.

### 💡 Why This Blog Exists

I’m writing here to:

- Document my dev journey - the **real stuff**, not just the highlights
- Share actionable tips on **frontend dev**, **freelancing**, and **mindset**
- Connect with other **self-taught devs**, **solo builders**, and **learners**

If this blog helps even one person code or think better, that’s a win. And if not - it still helps **future me** stay accountable.
